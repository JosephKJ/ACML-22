14th Asian Conference on Machine Learning


ACML 2022 aims to provide a leading international forum for Machine Learning researchers to share their new ideas and progress.

The conference will be conducted in a hybrid mode. Physical events would be hosted in Hyderabad, India.

Date: December 12-14, 2022
Venue: Indian School of Business (ISB), Hyderabad, India


Conference Schedule Accepted papers Workshops Tutorials Invited Speakers

About ACML 2022

    Welcome to ACML 2022, the 14th Asian Conference on Machine Learning, the premier international platform to bring together machine learning community. The conference aims to bring reserachers, engineers and practioners of machine learning and related fields to discuss and disseminate the innovations in the area of machine learning. ACML 2022 encourages significant and novel submissions to foster machine learning and adjacent domains. Following the success of previous ACML conferences, the conference will feature:
  • Keynote
  • Invited talks
  • Technical track
  • Tutorials
  • Mentoring sessions
  • Workshop program

    • Emerging out of pandemic era, we are adopting hybrid conference format, with a physical conference in Hyderabad, India along with a virtual mode for remote participation, while trying to bring best of both worlds.

    India, being a land of kaleidoscopic diversity and multicultural experiences has so much to offer. It is is a home to snow-covered Himalayas, architectural heritage, spectacular landspaces and tropical rain-forests. The detailed local information will be made available here later. Namaskar!

    We hope you have an awesome ACML 2022!
