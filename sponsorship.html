Call for Sponsorship – ACML 2022 and OAMLS@ACML 2022


This call for sponsorship covers both Asian Conference on Machine Learning (ACML) as well as the Online Asian Machine Learning School (OAMLS) co-organized with the conference. While the main conference paper presentations will remain virtual to encourage widespread participation in current times, the conference will also have physical components to allow in-person interaction for those who can attend. The sponsorship will be used to cover the costs of running the conference and the school, and will especially be used to attract keynote speakers, provide travel scholarships, and reduce the registration cost for participants – especially, those that attend in person. It will also help make the OAMLS School affordable for participants from under-represented areas in the Asia-Pacific region.

Impact and Visibility of Sponsorship
  • Sponsors will have an opportunity to have physical booths at the conference venue, as well as online publicity material (such as videos) in the virtual sessions, based on the sponsorship level.
  • Logos of sponsors will be displayed on the website as well as other online and printed publicity materials of the conference (according to sponsorship levels)
  • The audience for the sponsorship will include (overall number expected to be 300-400 or more):
    • physical participants at the conference,
    • virtual participants at the conference
    • OAMLS school participants (with permission of the presenters, lecture videos will be made publicly available for viewing, enhancing the reach to potentially many more interested students and practitioners over time)
  • Sponsors have the unique opportunity to recruit some of the brightest machine learning candidates in the region and to create brand awareness and name recognition among the machine learning talents and experts in the region and worldwide.

Specific levels and benefits are listed below:

Platinum - (US$ 6000 and above)
  • Largest logo placement on both conference and school webpages, as well as other online and offline conference publicity materials (including placeholder slides displayed during the conference and school sessions)
  • Six free registrations for the company representatives to attend both the school and the conference
  • Access to opt-in database of attendees who are interested in being recruited after conference and school registration
  • Large physical booth at the conference venue (subject to conducive conditions for physical conference and any social distancing requirements)
  • Display of longest possible promotional videos before online sessions of the conference
  • Invitation to present at an Expo/Industry session if organized (subject to content and speaker being appropriate)

Gold - (US$ 3000 - 5999)
  • Logo placement on conference and school webpages, as well as other online and offline conference publicity materials (including placeholder slides displayed during the conference and school sessions)
  • Three free registrations for the company representatives to attend both the school and the conference
  • Access to opt-in database of attendees who are interested in being recruited after conference and school registration
  • Small physical booth at the conference venue (subject to conducive conditions for physical conference and any social distancing requirements)
  • Display of short promotional videos before online sessions of the conference

Silver - (US$ 1000 - 2999)
  • Logo placement on conference and school webpages, as well as other online and offline conference publicity materials (including placeholder slides displayed during the conference and school sessions)
  • One free registrations for the company representatives to attend both the school and the conference
  • Access to opt-in database of attendees who are interested in being recruited after conference and school registration

For further discussion, please feel free to reach out to us at: acml2022-organizing-committee@googlegroups.com.
